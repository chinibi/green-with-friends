What is this?

The Greenest Badges Ever challenges everyone and their friends to go green. Users are presented with a set of challenges every week; when completed they will be awarded with a badge which is displayed in their profile.

Why are we doing this?

This website is about creating a culture of conservation, awareness, and teamwork in a planet impacted by human activity every day. Our focus is to educate each individual on what they can do right now, right there, and every day what they can do to preserve the home we all share. The Greenest Badges Ever is a way for people to show the role they play for the planet.

How do I participate?

Sign up

Create an account by clicking the Sign In button on the navbar.

Do the challenges

Check off the challenges as they are completed. When they're all done just hit the Finish button.

Feel good about yourself

Your new badge is displayed in your profile.

How can I participate with my friends?

Click the Friends button on the navbar and enter their username to send a friend request. When they accept you can track each others' badges earned.
